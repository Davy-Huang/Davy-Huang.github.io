Cell representations

As a research assistant at the Boston University Medical Campus studying glaucoma, I work with 3D models of cells. Represented is a cell within an aqueous flow pathway of the eye. Red indicates the cell itself. The blue and green are giant vacuoles within the cell.

Displayed is an overview of the methods utilized in acquiring and processing the data.


CAD Designs

Unmanned Aerial Vehicle-Skyhunter

The Skyhunter fixed wing was modeled as part of BU's UAV team. From this model, center of mass, airflow, and other features could be determined as components are added and removed.

Unmanned Aerial Vehicle-Bent Arm UAV

This model was designed as part of BU's UAV team. The 3D printed arms were evaluated to provide cushioning space between the motors/propellers and the ground during landings. Modular bars (attached in the image) were design to buffer the propellers against wall collision during flight.

Portal Gun
